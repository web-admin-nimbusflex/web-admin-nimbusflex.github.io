About

Nimbus™ II Flex is designed and made by the company InfuTronix. InfuTronix is an innovative medical device company offering cutting-edge technologies of infusion delivery systems for hospitals and home care environments. By combining data collection with portable, intuitive, easy-to-use infusion devices, InfuTronix is committed to improving the quality of patient care and assisting clinicians to deliver better patient outcomes. Headquartered in Natick, MA, InfuTronix specializes in the acute regional pain, epidural and PCA home infusion markets.
