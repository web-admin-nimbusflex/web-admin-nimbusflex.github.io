Customizable for Therapy Specific Applications

The Nimbus™ II Flex is a feature-rich, yet simple-to-use ambulatory infusion pump designed for repeated ambulatory continuous low volume infusion like chemotherapy. With an innovative and robust design, it comes pre-loaded with advanced therapeutic options, allowing you to tailor your infusion delivery to your patients' therapy. Nimbus™ II Flex is engineered to take continuous infusion therapy to newer and greater heights. Set your expectations higher and the future of infusion therapy is here.

AIR-FILTERED TUBING

Nuisance air-in-line and pump occlusion alarms are mitigated through a 1.2 micron air-eliminating filter in addition to sturdy tubing construction, letting patients rest at ease.

UNPARALLELED MOBILITY

Weighing only as much as your smartphone, Nimbus™ Flex doesn’t hamper you down. Feel free to go above your postoperative rehabilitation. 

INTEGRATED TECHNOLOGY

Integrated within the Nimbus Hardware, a micro USB port and a micro processor chip are two of the advanced technology options enabling Nimbus to be custom-configured.

CARRY CASE READY

Engineered with portability in mind, the battery-operated Nimbus™ Flex can be carried with you wherever you go and conveniently placed inside a custom carry pouch providing you full view of the InfuMed smart display screen.
